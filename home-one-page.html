With Proflex4, the new multivendor ATM software, banks can modernize their ATM user interfaces using HTML5.

Furthermore, Proflex4 offers the possibility of new service concepts as using smart phone instead of bank card for cash withdrawals.

The benefits of using Proflex4 are:

  • Consumer- Centric User Interface.
  • Multivendor software.
  • Open to next-generation services such as mobile transactions, personalized marketing campaigns.
  • Reduced costs for development, testing and deployment of ATMS.

Business Intelligence and Data Science powers business value in the following ways:

  • Empowering Management to make better decision.
  • Driving the business actions based on market trends through addition of statistics. The value is on recognizing trends that leads strategic decisions for innovation, new products and new services.
  • Fraud Detection and mitigation of risk is a main area of Data Science.
  • Personalization and Service: Big Data offers the opportunity of understanding to the customers through factors such real time- location, social media etc. to help to deliver personalization in the service.

How We Do.




ASA Tech Colombia is committed with the good practices in the process of development, Quality assurance and operations.

The Clean Architecture is our baseline to architecture our solutions based on separation of concerns.

Likewise, ASA Tech Colombia focus on on the good practices on software project management, leveraging on Scrum as Agile methodology through JIRA Software and Bitbucket as CSV centralized to manage our source code.

In regards operations, our incidents system is linked to JIRA to keep traceability of each issue in production and to be included in the tasks of the development team into Scrum.
